GOOGLE BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE ENGINEER - GROUP IS APPLICATION - (21000BLA)
 
Mission

GISACC is Valeo holding group IS application center in Cairo, where the company's digital transformation is taken to a new level. We aim to build efficient systems that incrementally add value everyday. Our BI engineers are part of a highly dynamic team that requires the design, development, testing & support for different business intelligence solutions to meet the identified business needs. Use effective data visualization and analytical skills to simplify complex business flows and enable faster decision making and execution
Build and maintain code base and business intelligence stack and continually innovate and introduce improvements
Identify and establish patterns and best practices for building excellent dashboards, reports and visual charts
